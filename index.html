Stellar Notifications

Get Real-time Notifications For Your Stellar Account!

Stellar Alerts is a notification system that alerts you whenever an operation has been carried out on your Stellar account. Start receiving these notifications by becoming a subscriber!

A simple guide for you...

1. Complete the subscription form.

2. Enter your public key and at least one method of receiving notifications, then click on the 'Subscribe' button.

Note that you can choose to receive notifications via all three methods.
